Game Instructions:

Mobile Controls:

  • Tilt your device left or right to move in that direction.
  • Swipe up anywhere on the screen to jump.
  • Tap the attack button or Tap anywhere on the game screen to attack and defeat enemies.
  • Tap Game Screen to Move to next level upon Level Completion
  • Tap Game Screen to restart level upon Game Over

Desktop Controls:

  • Use the Arrow Keys to move left or right and jump.
  • Press the SPACEBAR to attack enemies.
  • Press SPACEBAR to Move to next level upon Level Completion
  • Press SPACEBAR to restart current level upon Game Over

Enemy Descriptions and Attack Patterns:

Mardi Gras Zombie

Description:
A colorful zombie that walks towards the player to attack.

Origin:
Corrupted by Dark Matter from eatng King Cake before festival season

Attack Pattern:
Melee attack upon contact.

Behavior:
Slowly approaches the player and attacks directly. Can jump and change directions.

Trumpet Skeleton

Description:
A skeleton with a trumpet that jump towards the player blindly runs in the direction of the player.

Origin:
Corrupted by Dark Matter when seeking attention for their music before properly practicing

Attack Pattern:
Spawns, lunges at character and then charges at the player.

Behavior:
Initially jumps at player then run blindly in the direction of the player. Does not change directions.

Beignet Minion

Description:
Small enemy that shoots projectiles at the player.

Origin:
Corrupted by Dark Matter eating too many Cafe Du Monde beignets.
Now, fires beignets at will to infect others.

Attack Pattern:
Walks across the game screen and shoots projectiles at the player.

Behavior:
Moves horizontally and actively targets the player. Fires beignets at the player.
Player's projectiles destroy the beignet minion projectiles.

Beignet Monster

Description:
A larger enemy with 2 health that patrols an area.

Origin:
Corrupted by Dark Matter eating too many Cafe Du Monde beignets.
Now, wanders aimlessly through the French Quarter randomly attacking others.

Attack Pattern:
Causes damage on contact with increased health and increased damage to player.

Behavior:
Patrols randomly and withstands multiple hits.
Doesnt seem to know that the player exists. Destroy or stay out of the way.

Beignet Boss

Description:
The final boss with various attack phases.

Origin:
Corrupted by Dark Matter eating too many beignets from an unknown location.
Now, roams the area around Jackson Square seeking the ruin of souls.

Attack Pattern:
Shoots projectiles, summons minions, and has phase-based changes.

Behavior:
Adjusts attack patterns as its health decreases.
