Game Instructions:

Mobile Controls:

  • Tilt your device left or right to move in that direction.
  • Swipe up anywhere on the screen to jump.
  • Tap the attack button or Tap anywhere on the game screen to attack and defeat enemies.
  • Tap Game Screen to Move to next level upon Level Completion
  • Tap Game Screen to restart level upon Game Over

Desktop Controls:

  • Use the Arrow Keys to move left or right.
  • Press SPACEBAR to jump.
  • Press the SPACEBAR to attack enemies.
  • Press SPACEBAR to Move to next level upon Level Completion
  • Press SPACEBAR to restart current level upon Game Over

Tips:

  • Avoid obstacles and defeat enemies to progress through levels.
  • Keep an eye on your health bar and the number of enemies left.
  • Master your timing for attacks and jumps to avoid taking damage.

Enemy Descriptions and Attack Patterns:

Mardi Gras Zombie

Description: A colorful zombie that walks towards the player to attack.

Attack Pattern: Melee attack upon contact.

Behavior: Slowly approaches the player and attacks directly.

Trumpet Skeleton

Description: A skeleton with a trumpet that summons hazards or sound attacks.

Attack Pattern: Produces sound-based indirect attacks.

Behavior: Summons hazards or deals damage from a distance using sound.

Beignet Minion

Description: Small and fast enemy that shoots projectiles at the player.

Attack Pattern: Shoots projectiles in the player's direction.

Behavior: Moves horizontally and actively targets the player.

Beignet Monster

Description: A larger enemy with 2 health that patrols an area.

Attack Pattern: Causes damage on contact with increased resistance.

Behavior: Patrols specific areas and withstands multiple hits.

Beignet Boss

Description: The final boss with various attack phases.

Attack Pattern: Shoots projectiles, summons minions, and has phase-based changes.

Behavior: Adjusts attack patterns as its health decreases.
