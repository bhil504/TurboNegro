Game Instructions:

Mobile Controls:

  • Tilt your device left or right to move in that direction.
  • Swipe up anywhere on the screen to jump.
  • Tap the attack button or Tap anywhere on the game screen to attack and defeat enemies.
  • Tap Game Screen to Move to next level upon Level Completion
  • Tap Game Screen to restart level upon Game Over

Desktop Controls:

  • Use the Arrow Keys to move left or right and jump.
  • Press the SPACEBAR to attack enemies.
  • Press SPACEBAR to Move to next level upon Level Completion
  • Press SPACEBAR to restart current level upon Game Over

Tips:

  • Avoid obstacles and defeat enemies to progress through levels.
  • Keep an eye on your health bar and the number of enemies left.
  • Master your timing for attacks and jumps to avoid taking damage.
Warning!
IOS and Android users may not have the proper permissions for the tilt to move function to work.
In that case, please, use joystick for movement.

Enemy Descriptions and Attack Patterns:

Mardi Gras Zombie

Description: A colorful zombie that walks towards the player to attack.

Attack Pattern: Melee attack upon contact.

Behavior: Slowly approaches the player and attacks directly. Can jump and change directions.

Trumpet Skeleton

Description: A skeleton with a trumpet that jump towards the player blindly runs in the direction of the player.

Attack Pattern: Spawns, lunges at character and then charges at the player.

Behavior: Initially jumps at player then run blindly in the direction of the player. Does not change directions.

Beignet Minion

Description: Small enemy that shoots projectiles at the player.

Attack Pattern: Walks across the game screen and shoots projectiles at the player.

Behavior: Moves horizontally and actively targets the player. Fires beignets at the player. Player's projectiles destroy the beignet minion projectiles.

Beignet Monster

Description: A larger enemy with 2 health that patrols an area.

Attack Pattern: Causes damage on contact with increased health.

Behavior: Patrols randomly and withstands multiple hits. Doesnt seem to know that the player exists. Destroy or stay out of the way.

Beignet Boss

Description: The final boss with various attack phases.

Attack Pattern: Shoots projectiles, summons minions, and has phase-based changes.

Behavior: Adjusts attack patterns as its health decreases.
